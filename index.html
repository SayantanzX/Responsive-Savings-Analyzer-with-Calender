About us

We've Been Making Healthy Food For Last 10 Years

Welcome to Banerjee Restaurant in Panagarh a culinary gem where Bengali, South Indian, and Chinese cuisines harmoniously converge at reasonable prices. Our warm and inviting space promises an extensive menu curated for diverse palates, ensuring an affordable and delightful dining experience. Conveniently located, Banerjee Restaurant invites you to savor the rich tapestry of flavors, where affordability meets excellence in every dish. Join us for a journey through the best of Indian and Chinese gastronomy, all under one roof.

Food Menu

MUTTON KASHA (KOSHA MANGSHO)

Mutton Kasha is a delicious spicy Bengali Mutton Curry which is full of flavours from mutton, spices and mustard oil.

price: ₹ 250

PANEER BUTTER MASALA

Paneer Butter Masala is essentially one of the simplest yet satiating Indian accompaniment made using dollops of fresh cream, paneer and spices.

price: ₹ 250

CHICKEN BIRYANI

Best in town Dum Biryani served with mint chutney and raita.

CHICKEN CHAAP

Chicken Chaap is a classic delight for Indian food lovers. This is a unique combination of juicy and moist chicken pieces and rich and thick gravy.

price: ₹ 250

Idli

Idli Sambar is a South Indian breakfast meal where soft fluffy steamed cakes known as idli are served with sambar, a vegetable lentil stew. Idli Sambar is made with lentils, plenty of spices, mixed vegetables and herbs.

price: ₹ 250

Dosa

A dosa is a thin savory crepe in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice. Dosas are served hot, often with chutney and sambar. Dosas are popular in South Asia as well as around the world.

price: ₹ 250
